I used to try to read all books all the way through.

I realized that most books are not worth reading in their entirety.

Most books are full of filler and are dragged out to hit a page count.

Just read whatever you are interested in at the moment.

Don't show off books you are reading or your bookshelf.

The number of books you read means nothing.
