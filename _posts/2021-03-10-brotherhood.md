The only people that have ever hated me are females.

I've yet to have a friendship with a guy that went entirely south and stayed that way.

It's easy to create friendships with other men.

There is no beating around the bush, we can bond over anything.

The saying "All men are brothers" is timelessly true.

It is a fraternal feeling that can't be explained other than it is primitive in nature.

It's us versus the world.

Don't give up on the men in your community. We are strong when we are undivided.
